To view your stats, make a selection from the first "Select Function" drop down menu, then select a domain to view.

To view stats for yesterday, check the "Yesterday" checkbox.

NOTE: Because bandwidth stats already show for the month, the checkbox for "Yesterday" is non-functional with this selection.

When viewing realtime referral stats, be sure to choose the number of lines you wish to show.
